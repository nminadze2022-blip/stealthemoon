About the Demon Dogs

The Demon Dogs FTC team competes in FIRST Tech Challenge, where alliances of student-built robots race to score points in a two-and-a-half-minute game. We plan, prototype, CAD, wire, and program our robot from the ground up using the official FTC control system.

From autonomous paths that run on their own to intense driver-controlled matches, we learn real engineering, agile teamwork, and presentation skills as we document our work in an engineering portfolio and present to judges.

Our NYC-based team practices after school and on weekends, hosts STEM outreach events, and scrimmages with other local FTC teams. Qualifying for the World Championships is the result of thousands of hours of hard work—and now we need a village to help send us there.

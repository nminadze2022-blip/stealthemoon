What makes FTC special

In FTC, middle and high school students design, build, and program a 18" x 18" x 18" robot to play a new game each season. Robots run an autonomous mode, then drivers take over using gamepads to score as many points as possible.

Teams are judged not just on performance, but on their engineering portfolio, outreach, and Gracious Professionalism. Your donation helps us compete for awards like Inspire, Think, and Connect as we represent NYC on the world stage.
